Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Reddit Story Shorts Bot (the "Service") handles information when used by the account owner.

1. Information Collected

The Service may process:

  • OAuth authorization data for connected platform accounts (for example, TikTok).
  • Content and metadata needed to generate and upload videos.
  • Basic logs required for operation and troubleshooting.

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to:

  • Generate media content.
  • Upload approved content to connected accounts.
  • Maintain and debug the Service.

3. Sharing

Information is not sold. Data may be shared with service providers only as needed to operate the Service (for example, TikTok, YouTube, OpenAI APIs).

4. Retention and Security

Data is stored locally or in configured infrastructure and retained only as long as needed for operation. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no method of storage is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

You can revoke account access through connected platform settings and stop using the Service at any time.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: you@example.com
